Class of 2017 Graduation
May 16, 2017

On May 16 of this past year, 16 of our Brothers graduated from the Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy at Rutgers University. Over 25 of our collegiate and alumni Brothers showed up to cheer on the graduates as they walk across the stage to accept their diploma and shake hands with the Dean of Pharmacy. One of the keynote speaker at this ceremony was the famous Ernest Mario, who the school of pharmacy is currently named after.

The following are the graduates of the Class of 2017: Grace Chun, Nicole Daukshus, Samer Karam, Daisy Kim, Cassandra Leo, Jessica Li, Jesus Melendez, Lan-Anh Nguyen, Saba Nikpour,Samantha Paone, Daniel Park, Dev Patel, Shumaila Saeed, Courtney Tomaselli, Anna Yang, Yumi Yi
