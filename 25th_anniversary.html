Beta Nu's 25th Anniversary
April 23, 2017

The Beta Nu Chapter of Phi Delta Chi was founded May 2nd of 1992, making 2017 our 25th year anniversary. Our chapter hosted a banquet to commemorate the anniversary where Brothers from the Alpha Class to our most recent pledge class was in attendance. We also inducted the 2017 graduates into alumni of the Beta Nu Chapter.

The banquet began with introductions from the Worthy Alumni Liaison Peter lee. It was then followed by a speech given by the Worthy Chief Counselor Suny Kim where he reminisced his journey in the Brotherhood and shared a photo album about our chapter's charter back in 1992 that was saved by the Grand Poobah Ralph Saroyan. Other notable speakers were Brothers Nancy Palamara and Michelle Heyman from the Alpha Chapter. Our faculty advisor and Brother Daniel Abazia and Brothers Sejal Patel from the Alpha Omicron chapter were also in attendance. A major part of the banquet were the speeches given by littles who bigs were graduating that year. Tears were shed and laughter was shared throughout the speeches.
