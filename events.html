Events & Updates
September 16, 2017 - NERO

Every year, our collegiate chapter and the Beta Zeta Collegiate Chapter work together to host what is known as the North Eastern Regional Olympics, or NERO for short. This year, the event was held at Donaldson Park in Highland Park. Brother Min Kang led the victory for the relay race at NERO this year! Our Brothers were always at least one stage ahead of everyone else in the race.



September 13, 2017 - Alumni Panel

Every semester, the collegiate chapter hosts a variety of events to allow potential rushes to meet the Brothers. One of these rush event is the Alumni Panel where graduated Brothers come to talk to the rushes about their journey as a Brother of Phi Delta Chi. It is not only an event that is beneficial to the rushes, but it is also an opportunity for collegiate Brothers to connect with alumni that they may have never met or do not see very often.



August 27, 2017 - Lift Our Mugs Meet-Up

As we get busy with our own lives and responsibilities, we always remember to make some time for our Brothers. Several of our Brothers met up at ZeppelinHall Biergarten in Jersey City to catch up with each other as summer was coming to an end during the last week of August.



August 1-5, 2017 - 71st Grand Council

For the 71st Grand Council at Washington D.C, our chapter was able to send 9 collegiate Brothers to attend the conference and have two wonderful alumni as delegates. During the end of the week, over 25 more Brothers joined the attendees for the banquet portion and for some Brotherhood festivities.



May 16, 2017 - Class of 2017 Graduation

On May 16 of this past year, 16 of our Brothers graduated from the Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy at Rutgers University. Over 25 of our collegiate and alumni Brothers showed up to cheer on the graduates as they walk across the stage to accept their diploma and shake hands with the Dean of Pharmacy.



April 23, 2017 - Beta Nu's 25th Anniversary

The Beta Nu Chapter of Phi Delta Chi was founded May 2nd of 1992, making 2017 our 25th year anniversary. Our chapter hosted a banquet to commemorate the anniversary where Brothers from the Alpha Class to our most recent pledge class was in attendance. We also iniated the 2017 graduates into alumni.
