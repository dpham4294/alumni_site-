Events & Updates

North East Regional Conference
February 23-25, 2018

This February, 7 Beta Nu Brothers attended NERC hosted by the Beta Alpha chapter in Queens, NY. Thank you to our wonderful Alumni Brother Lloyd Alcala for taking the time out to join us on this fun weekend! The weekend started out strong, with around 20 more Beta Nu Brothers came up for the Friday night social and were able to meet Brothers from all over the Northeast Region.


Alumni Panel Rush Event
January 24, 2018

In the beginning of every semester, Beta Nu traditionally hosts an alumni panel event as a part of recruitment. At this panel, we invite our alumni Brothers to come back and share their wisdom and experiences. Through this panel discussion, both collegiate brothers and candidates learn how they can develop themselves as leaders in pharmacy school and achieve their career aspirations.


125th Anniversary & Pharmacy Building Reopening
October 6, 2017

On Friday, October 6th, the Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy celebrated the grand opening of the new-and-improved pharmacy school. The add-on to the original building is full of well-lit, open spaces and beautiful glass sculptures. After the ribbon-cutting, everybody was welcome to enjoy the delicious selection of food and drink. It was a classy affair, complete with a wine bar, multiple entree stations, and a dessert stand.


North East Regional Olympics
September 16, 2017

Every year, our collegiate chapter and the Beta Zeta Collegiate Chapter work together to host what is known as the North Eastern Regional Olympics, or NERO for short. This year, the event was held at Donaldson Park in Highland Park. Brother Min Kang led the victory for the relay race at NERO this year! Our Brothers were always at least one stage ahead of everyone else in the race.


Alumni Panel
September 13, 2017

Every semester, the collegiate chapter hosts a variety of events to allow potential rushes to meet the Brothers. One of these rush event is the Alumni Panel where graduated Brothers come to talk to the rushes about their journey as a Brother of Phi Delta Chi. It is not only an event that is beneficial to the rushes, but it is also an opportunity for collegiate Brothers to connect with alumni that they may have never met or do not see very often.
