Alumni Panel Rush Event
September 13, 2017

Pictured above are Brothers Vani Kumaran, Chang-Woo Han, Nilam Shah, Samer Karam, Sweta Chikoti, Catherine Wong, Diamond Pham, and Mingyang Lin.

Every semester, the collegiate chapter hosts a variety of events to allow potential rushes to meet the Brothers. One of these rush event is the Alumni Panel where graduated Brothers come to talk to the rushes about their journey as a Brother of Phi Delta Chi. It is not only an event that is beneficial to the rushes, but it is also an opportunity for collegiate Brothers to connect with alumni that they may have never met or do not see very often.

Over 30 rushes were in attendance for this event. Alumni were asked questions such as "what was your favorite PDC memory?" and "How did PDC help you get involved in other organizations?" by the Worthy Alumni Liaison Kelsey Lee. Towards the end of the event, rushes had a chance to directly ask our alumni Brothers any questions they had and to also socialize more casually with our alumni. Brother Nilam Shah had brought a fellow PDC Brother, Sweta Chikoti, to speak at the event. Sweta is a Beta Pi brother from Shenandoah University in Virginia and she is also Nilam's co-worker. It is amazing how often we encounter Brothers from other chapters within the workforce.
