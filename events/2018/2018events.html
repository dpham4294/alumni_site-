Events & Updates

North East Regional Conference
February 23-25, 2018

This February, 7 Beta Nu Brothers attended NERC hosted by the Beta Alpha chapter in Queens, NY. Thank you to our wonderful Alumni Brother Lloyd Alcala for taking the time out to join us on this fun weekend! The weekend started out strong, with around 20 more Beta Nu Brothers came up for the Friday night social and were able to meet Brothers from all over the Northeast Region.


Alumni Panel Rush Event
January 24, 2018

In the beginning of every semester, Beta Nu traditionally hosts an alumni panel event as a part of recruitment. At this panel, we invite our alumni Brothers to come back and share their wisdom and experiences. Through this panel discussion, both collegiate brothers and candidates learn how they can develop themselves as leaders in pharmacy school and achieve their career aspirations.


Let us keep burning the light of wisdom... Let us hear much, speak little, and act well... Let us be just...
