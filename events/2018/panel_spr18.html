Alumni Panel Rush Event
January 24, 2018

Pictured above are Brothers Kelsey Lee (WAL), Ted Ray, Pritha Chakrabarti, Michelle D'Andrea, Swati Shah, Parin Patel, and Kevin Chang.

In the beginning of every semester, Beta Nu traditionally hosts an alumni panel event as a part of recruitment. At this panel, we invite our alumni Brothers to come back and share their wisdom and experiences. Through this panel discussion, both collegiate brothers and candidates learn how they can develop themselves as leaders in pharmacy school and achieve their career aspirations. In our past event, alumni shared how their leadership experiences in PDC and other pharmacy organizations aided them in their everyday work environments from resolving conflicts to approaching other professionals.

“In PDC We Crust” sets a professional tone amongst candidates and allows them to realize how PDC can help develop them into leaders as well as more well-rounded individuals. It also lets collegiate brothers reflect upon how much they have progressed since freshman year and how much more they could grow as professionals and leaders because these alumni brothers are our role models. The name, “In PDC We Crust”, incorporates the trust we put into PDC as collegiate and alumni brothers and the pizza we serve at the event.
