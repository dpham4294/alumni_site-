A Little History Lesson

On May 2 1992, the Beta Nu Chapter of the Phi Delta Chi Pharmacy Fraternity was founded at the Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy at Rutgers University in New Jersey. Several passionate and distinguished alumni wanted to continue to foster and promote the brotherhood of Phi Delta Chi. This led to the official charter of the Beta Nu Alumni Chapter on June 25th of 2009.
