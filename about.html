A Little History Lesson

On May 2 1992, the Beta Nu Chapter of the Phi Delta Chi Pharmacy Fraternity was founded at the Ernest Mario School of Pharmacy at Rutgers University in New Jersey. Our Alpha Chapter consists of 32 Brothers whose names are recognized by each and every single generation of pledge classes that has crossed since the establishment of this chapter. The passion of several distinguished Brothers to foster and promote the brotherhood of Phi Delta Chi even after their collegiates years had lead to the official charter of the Beta Nu Alumni Chapter on June 25th of 2009.

Our Objective

The objective of this association shall be, but not limited to, promoting and advancing the profession of pharmacy, leadership, and its allied interests. We aim to do so by hosting professional and charitable events to support the community, as well as fostering camaraderie and fraternal spirit between Alumni Brothers, the Beta Nu Collegiate Chapter, and Phi Delta Chi as a whole.

The Founding Members of the Alumni Chapter are Brothers Louis An, Kailyn Archibald, Kaleen Barbary, Pritha Chakrabarti, Helen Chen, Jen Cimoch, Susan Craig, Renata Dul, Samantha Giniger, Melody Huang, Victoria Hui, Megan Kumpel, Bernie Lee, Kristen Moffa, Jennell Morin, Dalal Nesheiwat, Tonia Nesheiwat, Cate Nojiri, Monica Rivera, Moulakshi Roychowdhury, Ripal Shah, Shruti Shah, Joala Tubilleja, Diana Zisa.
